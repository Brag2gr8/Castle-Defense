CASTLE DEFENSE

The Castle of Camelot is under attack ⚔️⚔️

the wizard has to battle 3 monsters to save the castle. Roll the dice to decide winners

Game Rules

  • There are three(3) monsters in total to be defeated

  • On each dice roll, the added outcome is subtracted from each player's health

  • After the wizard defeats each monster, another appears till the three(3) monsters is defeated
  • if the wizard defeats all, the castle is safe and vice versa

  • if both wizard and monsters are defeated, the game ends in a draw
